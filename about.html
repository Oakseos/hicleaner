About Our Company

Cleaning can be a chore and we know you have many choices when you consider hiring a comprehensive, high quality, reliable cleaning service.

More than just cleaning services...

HiCleaners, born out of desire to solve hard matters was established for the service of cleaning of all types of properties regardless of size and need while paying attention to every little details. This quality has made us an effective cleaning force in the industry.

We are trained and experienced to serve with diligence and integrity in the line to achieve happy and satisfied clients through our array of services ranging from property cleaning (deep or standard), laundry (dry or wet), lawn/ Garden pruning and most especially fumigation.

HiCleaners offer services for residential, commercial/corporate, institutional and governmental properties ensuring that premises are clean, safe, healthy and efficient for the occupants.

Putting in mind your security and privacy, our staff are scrutinized and trained by our management to be committed and passionate, focusing on the goal of meeting your cleaning needs and to ensure your peace of mind.

We are very confident that with us at the helm of your cleaning affairs you can relax, enjoy and see your cleaning job done with professionalism and excellence.
