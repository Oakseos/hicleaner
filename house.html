Our Services

House Cleaning

Home cleaning could be very easy for some while it’s a herculean task for others, but either ways it’s surely a time taking activity if you are to achieve any form of satisfaction. At HiCleaners, we offer a full range of professional options, using top quality cleaning materials and Equipment, to achieve brilliant results for our valued customers.

Our home cleaning service packages are designed to give you the satisfaction you desire at a very competitive rate while you enjoy your time. Our packages for home cleaning are either standard or deep cleaning. These services come in flexible options of one-off, monthly, weekly, biweekly and regularly (daily).

    Home and Office services include:
  • Deep Cleaning
  • Standard Cleaning
  • Move in & Out Cleaning
  • Transfer Cleaning
  • Post Renovation/Construction

Office Cleaning
